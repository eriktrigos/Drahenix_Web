ABOUT.

Everything that you might be wondering about us in a short compress paragraph. Drahenix is a company that cares about solving day to day issues (sometimes too complex and some others too simple), and also bring the high tech concepts to the final users.

Drahenix was born in a call between 2 friends one Sunday at lunch. The topic was to write some code and do something fun, and after couple of hours, we decided to take it to the next level and let more people enjoy it. As result we created «drahenix» which is the combination of 2 words: dragon and phoenix with an «h» gluing it all together.

Bring technology to our day to day activities.

Living the present as the same way we visualize the future.
